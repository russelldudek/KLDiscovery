# KLDiscovery Brand Intelligence

## Candidate-use boundary

This is an independent candidate campaign by Russell Dudek. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by KLDiscovery.

## Authoritative references

- Current company experience and visual identity: https://www.kldiscovery.com/
- Company overview: https://www.kldiscovery.com/about
- Website intellectual-property terms: https://investors.kldiscovery.com/terms-of-use/default.aspx

## Company identity decision

KLDiscovery's public terms state that its logos and website content may not be incorporated into another website without express written consent. The campaign therefore uses a restrained, typeset nominative company identity above the fold and on document surfaces. It does not copy, trace, redraw, recolor, crop, animate, hotlink, or otherwise reproduce the corporate mark.

Status: official logo/wordmark unavailable for this use because of a documented source restriction.

## Color tokens

- `--kld-blue: #0067a9` - source-sampled approximation from the current public digital identity.
- `--kld-cyan: #00a5d8` - source-sampled approximation from current public digital accents.
- `--kld-navy: #071f33` - source-sampled dark surface aligned to the public site's technical/legal character.
- `--kld-slate: #334b5e` and `--kld-mist: #eef5f8` - source-sampled neutral system.
- `--signal: #e9a73f`, `--danger: #b8444f`, and `--success: #1d7a61` - candidate-original semantic colors for anomaly, hold, and resolved states.

The source-sampled recognition layer dominates. Candidate-original colors are subordinate and serve the Matter State Contract model.

## Typography

The current public site uses a modern, compact sans-serif system with bold geometric headings and clear operational body copy. No licensed font file is copied. The campaign uses Aptos, Segoe UI, Helvetica Neue, Arial, and generic sans-serif fallbacks, reproducing the public hierarchy through weight, spacing, compact headings, and restrained density rather than claiming an official typeface.

## Visual translation

- Company cues: blue/cyan technical precision, dark legal-data surfaces, high contrast, clear information hierarchy, and disciplined execution.
- Candidate-original role metaphor: a Matter State Contract whose clauses define state, evidence, ownership, SLA, authority, and client-safe visibility.
- Motion: a one-shot clause assembly that reaches a static verified state; scenario interaction changes explicit contract fields and exception states.
- Documents: conservative blue/cyan rules and type hierarchy; no fake corporate letterhead.

## Imagery

No company photography or proprietary interface capture is used. The campaign uses original diagrams and an original Matter State Contract symbol. The symbol supplements the operating model and does not replace the visible KLDiscovery typeset identity.
