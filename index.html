The real mandate

Convert service delivery into a trusted operating product.

KLDiscovery already combines technology, deep domain expertise, and integrated client services across complex matters. The role creates leverage by making the operating system behind that service visible, measurable, and progressively automatable.

Speed and scale

Eliminate manual handoffs, consolidate workflows, and move terabytes of legal data through the lifecycle faster.

Standardized product

Create common state, event, and integration patterns that compound across matters.

AI initiative

Use anomaly detection and agentic patterns where they can find risk earlier and reduce recurring work.

PRODUCT LEADERSHIP

Defensibility and precision

Preserve lineage, evidence, quality controls, and accountable human authority.

Matter-specific judgment

Support legitimate variation without rebuilding the operating model for every engagement.

Client trust

Expose useful truth without publishing noise, ambiguity, or a status reconstructed after the fact.

The Matter State Contract

One event model. Three product surfaces.

The contract is not legal boilerplate. It is the minimum operational record required to move a matter responsibly.

Workflow acts on the contract. AI tests the contract. The portal translates the contract.

Operating sequence: define expected state - observe actual state - detect divergence - assign human authority - preserve evidence - publish a client-safe update - learn from recurrence.

First 90 days

Learn the matter. Instrument the truth. Ship one closed loop.

DAYS 01-30

Read the operating system

  • Shadow matters across intake, collection, processing, review, production, billing, and client communication.
  • Map systems, event sources, handoffs, exception taxonomies, decision rights, and SLA definitions.
  • Baseline metrics without confusing available data with useful truth.
DAYS 31-60

Define the contract

  • Choose one high-value matter archetype and recurring exception family.
  • Co-design the minimum state, evidence, owner, authority, and client-safe visibility contract.
  • Prototype orchestration and anomaly logic with the AI Architect and Engineering.
DAYS 61-90

Close the loop

  • Pilot one end-to-end workflow with instrumentation, exception ownership, and portal translation.
  • Run pre/post evidence review across cycle time, quality, SLA, automation, and adoption.
  • Publish roadmap decisions: scale, revise, integrate, hold, or stop.
Executive interview questions

Questions that clarify the mandate.

Where do client expectations and the current internal matter state diverge most often-and how is that learned today?
Which workflow handoffs create the highest combination of cycle-time drag, defect risk, and client escalation?
What authority should remain with matter teams even after orchestration and anomaly detection mature?
How do Product, Client Services, Operations, Engineering, and the AI Architect currently split outcome ownership?
What would make this role an unmistakable success after twelve months: margin, speed, quality, portal adoption, trust, or a specific portfolio shift?
